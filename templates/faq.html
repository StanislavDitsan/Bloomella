Frequently Asked Questions


You can place orders through our website at any time of the day. Note: Orders will be processed during the working hours 09:00 - 20:00 UTC time zone.
Orders are processed upon receipt, and confirmation will be sent to the provided contact information.

Payment is required at the time of order placement.
All products displayed are subject to availability.

Orders can be canceled within 35min after placement.

Delivery charges apply and are outlined during the checkout process.

Delivery personnel will attempt to contact recipients before delivery.
